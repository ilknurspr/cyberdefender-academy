Enterprise Closing Principles
Never Discount Without Value Reframe

Price objections are about perceived value. Quantify cost of inaction before discussing pricing flexibility.

Trade, Don't Concede

Every discount must be exchanged for value: longer contract, faster decision, reference status, expansion commitment.

Quantify ROI for Economic Buyers

CFOs decide on quantified business impact. Use prospect's numbers: audit costs, insurance premiums, breach exposure.

Leverage Champions Against Procurement

Engage champions to reinforce value and expose gaps in competitive alternatives.
