Battle: CyberArk vs. HashiCorp Vault

HashiCorp Vault Price Sensitive DevOps Focused
Prospect Context: Mid-market fintech company evaluating PAM solutions. They're drawn to HashiCorp Vault's lower price point and familiarity with their DevOps tools.

Key Objections to Address:

  • "HashiCorp Vault is significantly cheaper than CyberArk"
  • "We already use HashiCorp tools in our infrastructure"
  • "Vault is more developer-friendly and easier to integrate"
  • "CyberArk seems overly complex for our needs"

Your Battle Card Talking Points:

  • Enterprise-grade security vs. secrets management tool
  • Compliance & audit capabilities (SOX, PCI-DSS)
  • 24/7 enterprise support & proven at scale
  • Privileged session management (not just secrets)
  • TCO analysis: hidden costs of DIY security

Battle: CyberArk vs. BeyondTrust

BeyondTrust Feature Comparison Incumbent
Prospect Context: Enterprise healthcare organization currently using BeyondTrust. Concerned about platform limitations but worried about switching costs.

Key Objections to Address:

  • "BeyondTrust already covers our basic PAM needs"
  • "The switching cost seems too high right now"
  • "We're familiar with BeyondTrust's interface and workflows"
  • "BeyondTrust offers similar features at comparable pricing"

Your Battle Card Talking Points:

  • Superior threat detection & analytics capabilities
  • Cloud transformation readiness (hybrid/multi-cloud)
  • Best-in-class credential rotation automation
  • Migration support & proven methodology
  • Gartner Magic Quadrant leadership position

Battle: CyberArk vs. Delinea (Thycotic)

Delinea Ease of Use Quick Deploy
Prospect Context: Manufacturing company needs quick PAM deployment. Attracted to Delinea's reputation for ease of use and faster implementation.

Key Objections to Address:

  • "Delinea has a reputation for being easier to deploy and use"
  • "We need something up and running quickly, not a 6-month project"
  • "CyberArk requires specialized expertise we don't have"
  • "The learning curve for CyberArk seems steep for our team"

Your Battle Card Talking Points:

  • Modular deployment options (start small, scale up)
  • SaaS offering for rapid time-to-value
  • Comprehensive training & enablement programs
  • Professional services & implementation support
  • Future-proof architecture for long-term growth

Battle: CyberArk vs. Okta PAM

Okta Cloud Native Identity Platform
Prospect Context: SaaS company already using Okta for identity management. Considering expanding Okta's capabilities instead of adding CyberArk.

Key Objections to Address:

  • "We already have Okta for identity - why add another vendor?"
  • "Okta's PAM integrates seamlessly with what we already use"
  • "Managing multiple vendors adds complexity and cost"
  • "Okta is more aligned with our cloud-first strategy"

Your Battle Card Talking Points:

  • PAM depth vs. identity breadth - specialized expertise
  • Advanced threat protection & session monitoring
  • Regulatory compliance certifications (FedRAMP, etc.)
  • Integration capabilities with Okta (best-of-breed approach)
  • Proven track record in securing critical infrastructure
