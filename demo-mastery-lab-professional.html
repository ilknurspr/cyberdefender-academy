"Thanks for taking the time to walk me through CyberArk. I'll be honest—we've looked at several PAM solutions, and I'm concerned about implementation complexity. Our team is already stretched thin. Feature Dump Product Tour Approach

"I appreciate your candor about implementation concerns—that's a critical consideration. Before I show you anything, I'd like to understand your current state.
